Dad's Stir Fry

Description

A quick and easy stir-fry dish.

Ingredients

  • Diced Chicken Breast
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Minced Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • All the Vegetables

Steps

  1. Brown chicken, then remove from pan.
  2. Sautee onion until translucent on high heat, add garlic and pepper flakes.
  3. Once the sharp frangrance of the garlic subsides, add vegetables.
  4. Stir
  5. Add soy sauce and vinegar while continuing to stir.
  6. Add chicken back to pan.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
