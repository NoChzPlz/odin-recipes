Papa's Red Sauce

Description

A traditional family recipe passed on through generations. A great dish year-round.

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, Diced
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Minced Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano, Basil, Salt, and Pepper
  • Red Wine
  • Canned Tomato

Steps

  1. Sautee onion until translucent, add garlic and pepper flakes.
  2. Once the sharp frangrance of the garlic subsides, add tomato paste, herbs, and spices.
  3. As the tomato paste begins to brown and darken, de glase with red wine.
  4. As paste forms add tomatoes.
  5. Crush tomatoes to release juices and stir. Add a half can of water.
  6. Bring to boil then lower temperature and simmer for 30 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with bread or pasta. Cheese optional.
